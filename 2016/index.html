The first big Java and JVM conference in Spain

3 days to share knowledge and experiences, to meet enthusiasts and geeks, and to learn about new technologies.

Barcelona will host this great event next 16th to 18th of June 2016.

The Barcelona Java Users Group is proud of holding this conference in our great city.

As you probably know, Barcelona is a beautiful city with plenty of places to visit and some of the best urban beaches in the world!

Do not miss the opportunity to participate in this event to immerse yourself in 3-days interesting talks on Java, JVM and opensource technologies.
